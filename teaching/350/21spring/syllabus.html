Course Description
In this course we study the theoretical framework of calculus, including the fundamental properties of real numbers, sequences, series, limits, continuity, uniform continuity, topology of the real numbers and metric spaces, compactness, derivatives, integrals. We will cover most of Chapters 3 - 8 of the textbook.

Homework
You will have to spend a substantial amount of time outside of class reading, thinking, and solving problems. Homework problems will require writing rigorous proofs. Although you are permitted to discuss homework problems with other students, I encourage you to try them by yourself first. You must write up your own homework solutions. Approximately 18 homework assignments will be collected. Selected problems will be graded.
