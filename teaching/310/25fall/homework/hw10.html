Homework 10: The Probability Density Function

  1. Section 3.3, #4, 5, 6, 8.
  2. The probability density function of \(X\), the lifetime of a certain electronic device (measured in hours), is given by $$ f_X(x)= \begin{cases} \frac{10}{x^2}, & x>10\\ 0, & x\leq 10 \end{cases} $$
    1. Find \(P(X>20)\).
    2. What is the probability that of 6 such devices at least 3 will function for at least 20 hours? What assumptions are you making?
